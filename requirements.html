Course Requirements

Every prerequisite you need before applying to medical school, organized by subject. Use the check boxes below to see if you have meet all the requirements! - Plan ahead

Biology

Two semesters of biology with lab are required by most medical schools.

Done Course Credits When
Biology I 4 cr Freshman Fall
Biology II 4 cr Freshman Spring
Genetics 3 cr Sophomore

Chemistry

General chemistry, organic chemistry, and biochemistry are all required.

Done Course Credits When
General Chemistry I 4 cr Freshman Fall
General Chemistry II 4 cr Freshman Spring
Organic Chemistry I 3 cr Sophomore Fall
Organic Chemistry II 3 cr Sophomore Spring
Biochemistry 3 cr Junior

Physics

Two semesters of physics with lab. Check your target schools for algebra-based vs. calculus-based requirements.

Done Course Credits When
Physics I 4 cr Sophomore
Physics II 4 cr Junior

Math & Statistics

Statistics is emphasized on the MCAT. Many schools also require calculus.

Done Course Credits When
Calculus I 3 cr Freshman
Statistics 3 cr Sophomore

Psychology & Sociology

The MCAT's Psych/Socio section makes these courses standard prerequisites at most schools.

Done Course Credits When
Intro to Psychology 3 cr Any year
Intro to Sociology 3 cr Any year
